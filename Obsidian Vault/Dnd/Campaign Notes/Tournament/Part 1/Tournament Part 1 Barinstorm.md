
- Players start by creating characters and working with me to learn the combat on foundry, that will be considered the session zero content.
    

	- Groups of three players will create characters, learn the VTT and do a short tutorial battle
	    
	
		- Three players vs ogre?
		    

- I will send out an announcement that shows the gist of each character
	

	- Class, name, quick story blurb, AI gen portrait
		

- If there is an odd number of players
    

	- Start with a free for all match, first player to die will not be included in the team matches. Last player to die will get bonus money that they can use in the underworld marketplace for items they can use later in the tournament
	    
	
		- First player to die will have a surprise chance to jump to the top of the roster if they are able to defeat BOTH players of the winning team after their last match. The 2 person team will not be able to rest before this match.
    

- If there is an even number of players, go straight to team selection
    
	- Team selection
	    
	
		- Players will be able to see the full list of available team mates, and will be asked to anonymously vote their preferred team mate
		    
		
		- If they both vote each other, they are team mates
		    
		- If multiple vote for one person, that person may choose between the ones that voted for them, highest amount of votes picks first, dice decide between ties of votes
	    

- Team matches
    

- Tournament of matches
    
- Randomly pick from a set of battlegrounds
    

- Each battleground should include hazards, interesting terrain, etc
    

- Once team matches are over, the team mates will have a surprise 1v1 against each other to determine roster order
    

### Between Matches

- Have a small gladiator camp with a shop, a few interesting characters, roleplay opportunities
    

	- Some roleplay encounters may have rewards
	    
	- The underworld is a dirty, unfair place and players may be rewarded for taking risks to ‘cheat’
	    
	
	- Think chunin exams 
    

- Encounter ideas
    

	- Beastmaster of the pit lost an important key in one of the dangerous beast cages. Who will retreave it for him?
	    
	
		- The key unlocks many beast cages and collars
		    
		- Beastmaster will reward with money
	    
	
	- Raining blood during a large battle in the mortal realm
	    
	
		- Fresh souls arrive in the pit, scared and ripe for befriending or taking advantage of
		    
		- An honorable knight who can't believe he wound up in the pit. “It must be some mistake!”
	    
	
	- An old hag who claims to be able to send a message to loved ones in the mortal realm.
	    
	- A recruiter for the pit mines
	    
	
		- The pit of the underworld is constantly shrinking due to encroaching formations of rocks, which need to be broken using hard manual labor
		    
		- The recruiter offers passage to a better plain of existence. “Now for only 5,000 years of service in the mines!”
		    
	
	- Hot daniel
	    
	
		- Players encounter a ripped halberd user, sharpening his weapon in the ready area. Shirtless and with majestic hair
	    
	
	- Lost member of the village of the blind
	    
	
		- Has wandered into the gladiator camp by mistake, is asking for help to return home
		    
		- Will reward players for returning him safely to the village of all blind souls
	    
	
	- Pit bard
	    
	
		- Players a jester tuning his lute and looking bored. He challenges them to a game of riddles
		    
		
		- If they answer three, he promises to buff them using a song in their next match.
	    
	
	- Illegal sports gambling
	    
	
		- Players will be given the opportunity to gamble their money on matches that they aren't taking part in.
