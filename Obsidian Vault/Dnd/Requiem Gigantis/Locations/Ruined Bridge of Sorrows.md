**The Ruined Bridge of Sorrows stretches across the Greenwater River half-collapsed and covered in creeping moss and vines. Its massive stone arches are now cracked and crumbling, with chunks missing that reveal the swirling, dark waters below. The bridge is still used by locals, though bringing a wagon across would be dangerous. A small path zig zags down the steep embankment. Below the bridge is an area with many stone pillars**
