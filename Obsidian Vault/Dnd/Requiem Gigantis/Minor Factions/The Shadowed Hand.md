- Shadowed Hand tactics and weapons

	- Many members of the Shadowed hand are actually empire veterans or deserters. 
	
	- What they lack in numbers they make up for in equipment, experience, and tactics.
	
	- Low level troops are hired thugs.
	
	- Actual members of The Hand will have beam weaponry, as well as blessed weapons and armor.
	
	- A favorite tactic is to use smoke bombs to retreat after inflicting some casualties.
	
	- They probably have a tunnel nearby to sneak into, and it's full of traps.
