**- Consists of many races, but founded by humans. Humans are in most of the higher positions.
    
- Not actually a state, but a merchant guild that has gained enough influence to have a significant presence in the region. 
    
- Many harbors are controlled by the federation. Local rulers allow them to conduct trade in their territory in exchange for a cut of the profit. The federation maintains a small navy to protect its assets but not a real military per say. 
    
- Power structure consists of [[harbor generals]] that elect a [[Spokesman]] who makes deals and maintains open lines of transportation between borders.
    
- [[Spokesman]] is a shrewd pragmatist who can convince enemies to indirectly trade with each other while profiting off of the process. 
    
- Often mistreats its workers in pursuit of higher profit margins. 
    
- Uses dirty tactics to maintain a monopoly on international trade.
    
- Will often fund rebel militia or fanatical groups in places that it deems unfriendly to the federation, secretly of course.
    
- Will employ [[adventurers guild]] as bounty hunters when people cross them. Occasionally using them to crush competitors.
    
- It is rumored that the federation’s central gold vault contains treasure beyond imagination.**

- Trade Federation tactics and weapons 

	- Smallest military, relies on logistical efficiency and speed of the navy. Will often employ mercenaries or hire guild parties for smaller jobs.
	
	- Guild captains will use horns and flags to call down cannon fire from nearby boats or communicate with other parties.
	
	- Every guild fleet has at least one stormcaller, a mage that uses thunder and wind magic.
	
	- Mercenaries use many types of weapons and tactics
