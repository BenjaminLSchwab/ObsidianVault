- Formerly nomadic, has recently conquered a significant chunk of the empire.
    
- Warrior culture. Wealth is often measured in livestock.
    
- Highly mobile armies thrive on the offensive.
    
- Leader is the [[High Urok]], granted his position from heaven. (inherited from father)
    
- Heaven has decreed that the Orcs will inherit all the land.
    
- Some areas are still nomadic, while others are subjugated people that used to be part of the empire.
    
- Nomadic tribes resent the wealthier settled tribes.
    
- Orcs are often granted land as payment for duties, which pushes the horde to constantly be conquering more.
    

- Orc Horde tactics and weapons 

	- Orcs usually ride large hogs with tusks, or sometimes horses. Use recurve bows to fire while mounted
	
	- Will retreat if they don't think they can win, fastest force on land.
	
	- Mish mash of weapons
	
	- For longer engagements or sieges, may employ large groups of goblins or conscripts from conquered lands.
