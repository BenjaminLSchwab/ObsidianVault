**- Consists of humans, halflings, gnomes, dwarfs.
    
- Is in the process of collapsing. Some pieces have already broken off. Other nations take bites out of its borders.
    
- Is host to the [[High Temple]] 
    
- Was founded when Elder Knowledge clerics discovered powerful artifacts shifting the balance of power, and allowing them to form their own state. 
    
- Ruled by a theocracy, the priest with the most seniority speaks for the church
    

- [[High Priestess Hera]] currently holds the seat of power
    

- At its core, the leadership of this nation uses technological relics to rule their area, pairing it with teachings derived from ancient texts to bind their people under one ideology. 
    
- They value giving everything for the temple, and putting the temple before everything else.
    
- Devout Clerics often command local militias and decide on disputes in temples.
    
- [[High Temple | The High Temple]] is said to be a place of great wonders, though few are allowed inside, and never outsiders.**

Holy Shard Empire tactics and weapons 

Advanced optics allow shard priests to concentrate light producing magic into beam weapons

Watch towers use mirrors to allow a seer to do recon in their areas. They prefer to fight on the defensive. 

When morale is low, shard priests will use blinding conviction, a spell that forces their followers to fight to the death

Regulars will use crossbows, swords, hammers

Veterans will sometimes carry blessed weapons and armor

Lowest level troops are just farmers with basic weapons
