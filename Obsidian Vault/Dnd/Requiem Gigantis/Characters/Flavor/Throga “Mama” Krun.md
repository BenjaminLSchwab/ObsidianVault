## Appearance
Towering half-orc with a patched apron, a club strapped to her back, and a baby strapped to her front. Always tired.

## Backstory
Her husband was drafted into a mercenary crew years ago and never came back. She runs a street food stall (“Krun’s Stewpot”) while wrangling her twelve children, all different shades of green.

## Example Quote
“I’ve changed diapers meaner than you. Now sit down, eat, and mind your manners before I tan your hide like it’s bedtime!”
