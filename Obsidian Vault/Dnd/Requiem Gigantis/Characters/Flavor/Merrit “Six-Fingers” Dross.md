## Overview
Race: Tiefling
Role: Gambling Master
Alignment: Chaotic Neutral
Personality Keywords: Flashy, persuasive, self-serving

## Description
A lanky Tiefling with crimson skin and curling ivory horns adorned with betting slips. His six-fingered hands are always counting coins, spinning dice, or adjusting wagers.

## Backstory
Once the most notorious bookmaker in the [[Pontis]] underworld, Merrit died when a mob boss drowned him in his own vault. He awoke in [[The Underworld]], and [[Veythar the Eternal|Veythar]] recognized a kindred spirit: a showman with a love for risk. Now Merrit takes bets from the dead, and ensures the odds always favor the house.
