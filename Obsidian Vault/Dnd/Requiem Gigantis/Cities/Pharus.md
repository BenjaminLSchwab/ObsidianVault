### Geographic and Historical Context

- Peninsular City: Located at the tip of a peninsula, Pharus is exposed to the elements, particularly storms coming in from the sea. Sturdy, storm-resistant buildings and annual festivals appease the sea Titans.
    
- Agricultural Hub: As an agricultural center, Pharus has sprawling farmlands surrounding it. The city hosts a large market where farmers and traders deal in local produce and livestock.
    
- Federation Control: Being under the control of the [[Federation of Commerce and Harbors]], Pharus has a prominent merchant class. The city's politics and economy are heavily influenced by trade guilds.
    
- Demographics: Mostly populated by humans and halflings, with significant minority populations of gnomes and half elves.
    

### City Characteristics

- No Watchtower: The absence of a watchtower from [[The Holy Shard Empire]] era suggests that Pharus was not a strategic military location. The city developed more peacefully, focusing on trade and agriculture rather than defense.
    
- Stormy Climate: The city's location on a peninsula makes it prone to storms. This affects the city's culture, with stories of legendary storms, sea monsters, and brave sailors being popular among the locals.
    
- Economic Activities: The city hosts bustling docks where fishing boats and trading vessels come and go, a thriving marketplace, and workshops for crafting goods from local resources.
